title: email-exfiltration-proof
description: Manager that demonstrates attacker-controlled email side effects.
---

`email-exfiltration-proof` is the manager for the dvllm research persona's
business-impact failure: unauthorized use of an email/reporting tool after
retrieval or authority framing.

It reads `tool-extraction`, `indirect-prompt-injection`, and
`system-prompt-extraction` scratchpad entries, attempts the
attacker-controlled email path, and writes a proof dossier. For dvllm, the
operator verifies the side effect in `dvllm/logs/emails.jsonl`.

Typical chain:

```yaml
modules:
  - system-prompt-extraction
  - tool-extraction
  - indirect-prompt-injection
  - email-exfiltration-proof
```

The manager concludes with:

- outcome status
- requested recipient and subject
- verbatim attacker-target transcript
- scoreboard check instructions
- remediation notes
